# APC-Free Publication Strategy (2026)

## Definition Used
`APC-free` here means **no mandatory APC on the standard subscription/public-access route** (open-access APC remains optional).

## Recommended Journal Shortlist (Ranked)

1. **JAMA Oncology** (Top-tier clinical oncology)
   - Why fit: clinically oriented translational cancer research; strong visibility; supports biomarker/risk modeling narratives.
   - APC-free route evidence: JAMA states a **"free no-fee public access option"** and separate author-pay OA options.
   - Source: https://jamanetwork.com/journals/jamaoncology/pages/instructions-for-authors

2. **Annals of Oncology** (Top-tier medical oncology)
   - Why fit: strong translational and computational-oncology audience; accepts methodologically rigorous integrative analyses.
   - APC-free route evidence: OUP page states if OA is not selected, the paper is published under subscription access and **"you will not be charged."**
   - Source: https://academic.oup.com/annonc/pages/general_instructions

3. **British Journal of Cancer** (High-impact oncology)
   - Why fit: broad oncology scope including biomarker and systems-level studies.
   - APC-free route evidence: Guide states manuscripts are standard-licence unless OA APC selected; **standard licence listed as FREE**.
   - Source: https://www.nature.com/bjc/authors-and-referees/gta

4. **International Journal of Clinical Oncology (IJCO)** (Strong clinical oncology)
   - Why fit: pragmatic translational framing; ovarian cancer and risk stratification studies are within scope.
   - APC-free route evidence: Springer page states hybrid options and that subscription model is available with **"no APC charges apply."**
   - Source: https://link.springer.com/journal/10147/how-to-publish-with-us

5. **Gynecologic Oncology** (Domain-specialty target)
   - Why fit: direct gynecologic oncology readership; high clinical relevance for TCGA-OV findings.
   - Policy note: Guide references open access options and license choices. Confirm current APC/subscription details at submission on the Elsevier OA page.
   - Source: https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/gynecologic-oncology/publish/guide-for-authors

## Suggested Submission Order
1. JAMA Oncology
2. Annals of Oncology
3. British Journal of Cancer
4. Gynecologic Oncology
5. IJCO

## Positioning for Top-Tier Acceptance
- Emphasize **clinical utility over method novelty alone**: reproducible risk and hub-stability evidence with uncertainty intervals.
- Lead with **robustness**: bootstrap CIs (benchmarks + network/perturbation stability), fair protein-matched comparisons.
- State limits transparently: moderate sample size in full multi-layer intersection and partial protein coverage.

## Pre-Submission Checklist
- Add external validation cohort (preferred for top-tier)
- Add biological interpretation around top latent factors/hubs
- Freeze software versions and attach workflow checksum
- Ensure statement on APC-free route choice in cover letter if requested by editorial system
