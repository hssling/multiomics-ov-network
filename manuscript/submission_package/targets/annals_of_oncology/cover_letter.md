Please consider our manuscript, "Robust Multi-Omics Network Integration in TCGA Ovarian Cancer: Bootstrap-Calibrated Prediction, Hub Stability, and Perturbation Evidence," for publication in Annals of Oncology.

We present an end-to-end reproducible integrative framework over TCGA-OV with mutation, CNA, methylation, RNA, and optional protein layers. The study combines latent-factor integration, supervised risk modeling, multilayer network centrality, and perturbation analysis, with bootstrap confidence intervals and rank-stability metrics.

Highlights:
- Core matched cohort n=90; protein-matched fairness subset n=57.
- Cross-model benchmark reporting AUC, C-index, and Cox C-index with 95% bootstrap CIs.
- Stable top hubs (LF8, LF5, LF6, LF7) with high top-k persistence.
- Perturbation effect intervals and rank stability for top candidate hubs.

We believe this work offers translationally relevant and methodologically rigorous evidence suitable for Annals of Oncology readers.
