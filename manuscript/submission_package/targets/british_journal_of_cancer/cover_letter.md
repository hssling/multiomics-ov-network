We submit our manuscript, "Cross-Layer Hub Stability and Risk Modeling in TCGA Ovarian Cancer Using a Reproducible Multi-Omics Network Pipeline," to the British Journal of Cancer.

Our study integrates multiple molecular layers in TCGA-OV and quantifies uncertainty at each core analysis step, including predictive benchmarking, network hub ranking, and perturbation effects. We report stable latent-factor hubs, fair protein-matched comparisons, and confidence intervals for model discrimination and survival concordance.

The work is designed for direct reproducibility and extension to independent cohorts, which we believe is aligned with BJC’s translational oncology scope.
