# BJC Submission Compliance

- Journal page checked: British Journal of Cancer Guide to Authors and publication policy pages on Nature Portfolio.
- Target article type: Article.
- Word target: BJC allows up to 5,000 words excluding abstract, references, and figure legends.
- Abstract: structured abstract, maximum 200 words, with Background, Methods, Results, Conclusions, and trial registration if applicable.
- Figure/table target: maximum 6 combined in the main paper.
- References: Vancouver style; list all authors up to 6, then first 6 plus et al.
- Required Additional Information sections included in order: Acknowledgements; Authors' contributions; Ethics approval and consent to participate; Consent for publication; Data availability; Competing interests; Funding information.
- Title page expectations: title, authors, affiliations, corresponding author email, ORCID.
- Peer review model: single anonymized.
- Publishing model: standard licence free; open access optional with APC; subscription route available without APC.
