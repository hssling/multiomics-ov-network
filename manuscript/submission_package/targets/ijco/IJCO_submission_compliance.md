# IJCO Submission Compliance

- Journal page checked: International Journal of Clinical Oncology submission guidelines and publishing page on Springer Nature.
- Article type used: Original article.
- Word target: IJCO states original articles should not exceed 3,000 words excluding abstract, references, tables, and figure captions.
- Abstract: structured, four headings, target within 250 words.
- Keywords: 3-6 keywords included.
- Main text structure: Introduction; Patients and Methods; Results; Discussion.
- Peer review model: single-blind.
- Publishing model: hybrid; subscription route available with no APC, open access optional with APC.
- Required declarations included: conflict of interests, data availability, ethics/informed consent statement, author contribution statement.
- Remaining submission items outside the manuscript file: Springer COI form and any journal-specific forms at submission.
